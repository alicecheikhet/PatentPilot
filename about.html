Why PatentPilot is different

PatentPilot is an inventor-side platform. It is built to help shape a concept before formal drafting: what is new, where overlap appears, how the claim should be structured, and what filing path makes sense next. It is not branded around examination, rejection, or government review workflows.

Suggested positioning

Use PatentPilot as the inventor-side complement to any separate patent review or legal analytics project.

  • Frame it as a tool for inventors and attorneys to shape patentable concepts before formal drafting.
  • Emphasize its editable patent sheet database, claim scaffold generation, and filing strategy memo.
  • Keep the branding product-oriented and innovation-oriented rather than examiner-oriented.
