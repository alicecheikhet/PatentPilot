PatentPilot

How to use:
1. Open index.html or run a simple local server.
2. Go to Idea Studio.
3. Enter your own matter, or click Load Sample Matter.
4. Click Run Analysis.
5. Review the Dashboard, Claim Architect, Novelty Radar, and Filing Strategy pages.
6. Update the Patent Sheet Database any time.

Everything is local and browser-based.

7. The Dashboard and Novelty Radar now include a closest-patent comparison card with a similarity score and patent link.

8. Dashboard and Novelty Radar show the top 3 similar patents with working Google Patents links.
